CONFIGURATION DETAILS
================================================================================

VALID DELAY OPTIONS:
- 500 milliseconds
- 1 second (default)
- 2 seconds
- 3 seconds
- 4 seconds
- 5 seconds

VALID HOURLY LIMITS:
- 10, 20, 30, 40, 50 (default), 60, 70, 80, 90, 100

STORAGE (Chrome Storage):
- delayInterval (int): Current delay in ms
- hourlyLimit (int): Current hourly limit
- sessionStartTime (timestamp): When session started
- isRunning (boolean): Session state
- totalAccepted (int): Lifetime counter
- hourlyAccepted (int): Current hour counter
- lastResetTime (timestamp): Last reset time
